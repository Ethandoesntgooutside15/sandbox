5 Most Powerful Marvel Characters


Number 1: Infinity Ultron

this is the episode from the TV series "What If" where ultron won

This is a hypothetical version of ultron if he entered the vision robot and used it to steal the infinity stones from thanos when he arrived on earth. He has enough power to leave the multiverse and he has even fought a watcher. He can use the reality stone to create millions of drones that are perfect copies of himself.


Number 2: The Maker

-art made by Adam Kubert-

This is Reed Richards from earth 1610, also known as The Maker. He became evil after kang the conqueror showed him the fate of the universe, which caused him to try to start saving it by any means necessary. He at one point gained cosmic omnipresence, which allows him to exist in all universes at once.
